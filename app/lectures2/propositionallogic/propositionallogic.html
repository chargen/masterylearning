Propositional Logic

What you will learn here

This topic will teach you the difference between syntax and semantics of propositional formulas. After completing this unit you will be able to distinguish syntactically incorrect formulars from correct ones. You will be able to evaluate well-formed formulas and you will know what it means if a logical formular is 'valid' or 'satisfiable'.
