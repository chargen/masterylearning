Step-wise formula evaluation

Mark tried to solve the following formula: `(a ^^ b) vv (c o+ d)`, given the following variable assignments, specify where mark has made a mistake.

  • `a rarr 0`
  • `b rarr 1`
  • `c rarr 1`
  • `d rarr 1`

Did Mark make a mistake?

`(a ^^ b) vv (c o+ d)`
Original Formula
`(0 ^^ 1) vv (1 o+ 1)`
Step 1
`1 vv (1 o+ 1)`
Step 2
`1 vv 0`
Step 3
1
Step 4
Mark did not make a mistake.

Answer is correct.

`o+` 0 1

Storyboard

Goal: This exercise type is useful to understand where a student has problems with the evaluation of a formula.

Assuming the student choose Step 3 then we know two things:

  • The student did not catch the mistake in Step 2, so she probably has a problem
    understanding the `^^` operator.
  • She thinks that the evaluation of `(1 o+ 1)` to `0` is wrong, which means she does not understand the `o+` operator.

Usage: Please select step 3.
